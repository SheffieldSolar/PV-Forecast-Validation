Sheffield Solar PV forecast validation tool

A CSV file with three columns:
  • forecast_base: Formatted as yyyy-mm-dd HH:MM:SS UTC
  • horizon: Forecast horizon as integer number of half-hours i.e. if the forecast_base is 07:00 UTC then a horizon of 1 refers to the interval 07:00 - 07:30
  • generation_mw: Forecasted generation in MW
